Mac and Cheese

Ingredients


1/2 pound macaroni
1 tsp butter
1 beaten egg
1 tsp salt
1 tsp dry mustard
3 cups sharp grated cheese
1 cup milk

Instructions

1) Boil macaroni in water until tender and drain thoroughly.
2) Stir in butter and egg
3) Mix mustard and salt with 1 tbsp hot water and add to milk.
4) Add cheese, leaving enough to sprinkle on top
5) Pour into buttered casserole, and milk, sprinkle with cheese.
6) Bake at 350Â°F for about 45 minutes or until custard is set and top is crusty.
