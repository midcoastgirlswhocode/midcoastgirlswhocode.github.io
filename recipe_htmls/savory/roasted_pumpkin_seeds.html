Pumpkin Seeds

Ingredients


2 cups pumpkin seeds
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
3 tbsp melted butter
1 tsp salt

Instructions

1) Rinse pumpkin seeds until pulp and strings are washed off
2) Boil seeds in salt water for 10 minutes. Dry
3) In medium bowl, combine Worcestershire sauce, melted butter, and salt. Add seeds
4) Stir until seeds are coated. Spread on baking sheet
5) Bake 1-2 hours at 225Â°F 6) Stir occasionally to prevent burning. Seeds should be crisp
