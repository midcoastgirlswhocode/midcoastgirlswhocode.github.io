Cookie Dunkers

Ingredients


1/2 cup chocolate fudge ice-cream topping, heat if desired.
1/2 cup butterscotch or caramel ice-cream topping, heat if desired.
1/2 frozen then thawed whipped topping
12 cookies of any flavor, about 2 1/2 inches in diameter

Instructions

1) Place ice-cream toppings and the whipped topping in separate small shallow bowls.
2) Dip cookies into ice-cream topping, then into whipped toppin.
