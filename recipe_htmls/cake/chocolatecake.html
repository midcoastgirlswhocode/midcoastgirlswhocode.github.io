Chocolate cake

1/2 cup of butter
4 squares of melted chocolate
2 cups of sugar
1 1/2 cup of milk
2 cups of flour
2 eggs seperated
2 teaspoons of baking powder
1/2 teaspoons of salt
2 teaspoons of vanilla
1 cup of chocolate
           Mix together milk, sugar, melted chocolate, and butter. In a larger, seperate 
  bowl mix together flour, baking powder, salt, and vanilla. Combine the mixtures in 
  the larger bowl. Fold in the stiffly beaten egg whites and bake in a 13x8x2 pan at 
  350ยบ for 35 minutes. Serve with chocolate ice cream and marshmellows.
