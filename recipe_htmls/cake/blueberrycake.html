Blueberry Cake

2 seperated eggs
1 cup of sugar
1/2 cup of shortening
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teasoon of vanilla
1 1/2 cup of flour
1 teaspoon of baking powder
1/3 cup of milk
1 1/2 cup of fresh or frozen blueberries
      Beat egg whites until stiff, and add 1/4 cup of sugar, cream the shortening, 
  then mix together the rest of the sugar, salt, vanilla, and add the egg yolks. 
  Beat until creamy, add flour, sifted with baking powder, alternatly with the milk
  Fold in egg whites, then sprinkle flour over the berries and fold into the batter
  into a well greased 8x8 pan, and sprinkle top with some sugar. Bake at 350ยบ for 50 
  minutes.
