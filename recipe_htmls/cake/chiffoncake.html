Chiffon cake

1 cup of flour
3/4 cups of sugar
1/4 cup of cooking oil
1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
1/2 teaspoon of salt
3 egg yolks 1/3 cup of cold water
1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
2 teaspoon lemon rind
2 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 cup of egg whites
1/4 cup of cream of tartar
         Sift flour, sugar, baking powder and salt into a large mixing bowl. Then in a 
seperate bowl mix together cooking oil, egg yolks, water, vanilla, lemon rind & juice. 
Beat with a wooden spoon until smooth. Measure the egg whites into a large bowl 
and add cream of tartar and whip until stiff. Fold in egg yolk mixture gradually until 
fully blended. Pour mixture into an unguarded 9ยบ cake pan and bake at 325 for 50-55
minutes, or until delicately browned.
